The mystery is solved: my favorite sport of the moment is... Pádel!




"Pádel" is a racket sport where the ball can hit the walls of the court (see image above) and your objective is to win points just as you do in tennis: if the ball hits your opponent's side floor twice or if they fail to make it hit on your side of the floor, you win the point.


The rackets are hard and the game it's pretty fast, so it's incredibly fun to play. I started playing in 2021 and, ever since, I've become a huge fan of it!
